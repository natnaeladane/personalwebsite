Short Biography


Natnael adane was born on January 31, 1998 from his father adane berhe and his mother emebet g/tsadik in the capital city of Ethiopia Addis Abeba specially known as winget-tero.natnael adane attend his kg, primary and secondary class at kokeb school and his preparatory class at near by schools called imperial academy, Africans dream school and bashewam school. after completing his highschool he attended Addis Ababa University and start studying information science bachelor of degree. He starts practicing computer works since he was child and he joined the art world by participating in different school events. At the age of 20 after being inspired by his role model Adonic Worku the hard working natnael adane founded his private company called Elana Printing and advertising with his co-owner, which is established in Addis Ababa to bring high quality works on printing, advertising, promotion, event organizing and also which works in entertainment business sector with respect to customer satisfaction. Because of natnael have creative mind and high communication skills he is lovable by his friends and his business partners. He is currently advising enterprunners in his telegram channel called NaelEvents.
